Job Specifications

Qualifications

To be considered for this role you need to meet the following requirements:

- Have a full UK driving licence

- Have at least 5 grade 4s or above at GCSE level, including English Language and Mathematics

- Have experience in web development and a basic understanding

- x3 Merit grades or above at A Level in any subject (ideally IT based)

How do i apply?

To apply for Hot Bean Web, you need to have completed training courses or qualifications related to web development, and looking for a job as a web developer.

If you are interested click on the Application form in the top left corner.
